What is RefundHelp?

RefundHelp is a professional service that helps you claim refunds for flights, subscriptions, purchases, and bank fees. We handle the entire process on your behalf, from submission to resolution.

How does "No Win, No Fee" work?

You only pay if we successfully recover your refund. There are no upfront costs. Our success fee (typically 20%) is deducted from the final refund amount sent to you.

How long does it take?

Flight claims usually take 2-4 weeks depending on the airline. Subscription refunds are often faster (7-14 days). We'll keep you updated at every step.

What evidence do I need?

Usually just a booking reference, receipt, or bank statement transaction. Our system will guide you through exactly what's needed for your specific claim type.
