Our Story

Empowering consumers to reclaim what's theirs.

Why we started

Millions of pounds go unclaimed every year because the refund process is intentionally complex. Airlines, banks, and subscription services count on you giving up.

Founded in 2023 by legal and tech experts, Refundly Pay automates the bureaucracy. We handle the paperwork, the legal letters, and the negotiations, so you can just sit back and get paid.
